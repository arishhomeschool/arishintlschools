Dr. Oluwagbenga, the Director of the Arish Group of Schools is a academic, astute writer, publisher, resercher and Professional Clinical Pharmacist.. He was educated at the Abeokuta Grammar School, Obafemi Awolowo University and University of Ibadan where he bagged his M.sc and Ph.d degree in Pharmacy with bias in pediatric specialty. A widely travelled man, dr Oluwagbenga Odunfa is a fellow of and a lecturer at the West African Post Graduate College of Pharacists.

His passion for education made him to establish Arish group of schools with his loving wife. Mrs Oluwayemisi Odunfa in the year 2012. he is a recipeint of several awards of both national and international repute.

He belongs to a number of professional organizations and the 100/10 Academy Club, Abeokuta branch.
